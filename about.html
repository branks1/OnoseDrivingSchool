ABOUT US

Onose Driving School is a reputable establishment where beginners, intermediates, and advanced drivers come to get the most comprehensive and effective driver training in Benin. If you want to get the best driver training experience out there, look no further, our world-class instructors will take you by the hand and guide you every step of the way. We are firmly committed to serving clients from any demographic and making sure that they get the skills and technical know-how to become responsible and safety-conscious drivers. To crown it all, our services are very affordable; you can’t get a better deal elsewhere.

Getting your driver’s license doesn’t have to be an arduous process. With the right training and guidance, you can get your own driver’s license in no time. You don’t have to keep tabs on the amount of time you spend in classroom and in-car sessions. All you need to do is focus on the learning, we can handle the rest. Our instructors are fun to be around so there is never a dull moment.

Every client is important to us; we pay unique attention to each and every one of them. Whatever your aim or goal for getting a driver’s license, we are at your service.

We have a solid slate of services, expertly curated to cater to the driving training needs of any individual. Our classroom sessions are top-notch, key principles are taught in a simple to grasp manner. For our in-car sessions, safety is key; you will be supervised by a licensed instructor. Our services include the following,

  • Instruction permit prep.
  • Teen program.
  • Adult program.
  • Defensive driving.
  • Individual lessons.
  • Stick shift lessons.
  • Chauffeur license training.
  • Motorcycle program.
  • Highway, parking and winter driving training.
  • Commercial driver’s license training.

Learning to drive and getting your driver’s license couldn’t be easier. Onose Driving School is at your service 24/7.
